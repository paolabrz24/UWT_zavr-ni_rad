Engineering Services

At the forefront of robotics innovation, we offer a comprehensive suite of advanced engineering services designed to support research, development, and performance optimization.

Our state-of-the-art facilities and cutting edge tools empower engineers and developers to push the boundaries of automation and intelligent systems.

Our services include:

Service Description
Computational Fluid Dynamics (CFD) High-precision simulations for analyzing airflow and thermal effects on robotic systems.
Simulator Real-time environments for testing control systems, autonomous behavior, and system integration.
Seven Post Test Rig Simulates dynamic forces to evaluate mechanical responses and durability under real-world loads.
Brake Dyno Performance testing for robotic braking systems and actuator reliability.
Tire Modeling and Test Services Custom modelling and testing for wheeled robots to ensure optimal grip, wear, and performance.
Wind Tunnel Aerodynamic testing for drones and mobile robots to enhance stability and efficiency.
