Engineering Services

At the forefront of robotics innovation, we offer a comprehensive suite of advanced engineering services designed to support research, development, and performance optimization.

Our state-of-the-art facilities and cutting edge tools empower engineers and developers to push the boundaries of automation and intelligent systems.

Computational Fluid Dynamics (CFD)

Our CFD services provide high-fidelity simulations of fluid flow and thermal dynamics, essential for optimizing robot performance in various environments. We use ANSYS Fluent and OpenFOAM for accurate results.

Simulator Testing

Real-time simulation environments that allow for comprehensive testing of control algorithms and autonomous behaviors before physical prototyping.

Seven Post Test Rig

Advanced mechanical testing platform that simulates real-world dynamic loads to validate structural integrity and durability of robotic systems.

Brake Dyno Testing

Precision testing of braking systems and actuators under various load conditions to ensure reliability and performance.

Tire Modeling Services

Custom tire models and testing for wheeled robots, including terrain interaction analysis and wear pattern prediction.

Wind Tunnel Testing

Aerodynamic performance evaluation for drones and mobile robots, with wind speeds up to 200 km/h and full instrumentation.

Our services include:

Computational Fluid Dynamics (CFD) High-precision simulations for analyzing airflow and thermal effects on robotic systems.
