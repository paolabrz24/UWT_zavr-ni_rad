History of our making

Momentum Solution International (MSI)

Founded on April 28th, 2025 by visionary entrepreneur Paola Brzoja, Momentum Solution International (MSI) was created with a bold mission: to reshape the future of human mobility, capability, and innovation.

At MSI, we are pioneers in developing next-generation prosthetic legs, arms, and spinal solutions, giving people not just functionality, but freedom, strength, and confidence. Driven by a deep commitment to human potential, we combine advanced robotics and cutting-edge artificial intelligence to create products that don't just replace — they empower.

From revolutionary prosthetics to intelligent robotic systems, our innovations are designed to push the boundaries of what's possible. Every creation at MSI embodies our core belief: technology should enhance life, restore dreams, and move humanity forward.

Headquartered globally, our multidisciplinary team of engineers, scientists, and visionaries work tirelessly to deliver solutions that are smart, adaptive, and deeply human-centered.

At Momentum Solution International, the future isn't just coming — we are the future of technology inspired by old designs. The past is history. The present is past. The future is now.
